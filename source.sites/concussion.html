Concussion

Storyline

Will Smith stars in Concussion, a dramatic thriller based
on the incredible true David vs. Goliath story of American immigrant Dr. Bennet
Omalu, the brilliant forensic neuropathologist who made the first discovery of CTE,
a football-related brain trauma, in a pro player and fought for the truth to be
known. Omalu's emotional quest puts him at dangerous odds with one of the most
powerful institutions in the world.

Director: Peter Landesman

Writers: Peter Landesman, Jeanne Marie Laskas (based on the GQ article "Game Brain")

Stars: Will Smith, Alec Baldwin, Albert Brooks

Release Date: 10 March 2016
