Seven Pounds

Storyline

Haunted by a secret, Ben Thomas looks for redemption by
radically transforming the lives of seven people he doesn't know. Once his plan is
set, nothing will be able to stop him. At least that's what he thinks. But Ben hadn't
planned on falling in love with one of these people and she's the one who will end
transforming him.

Director: Gabriele Muccino

Writer: Grant Nieporte

Stars: Will Smith, Rosario Dawson, Woody Harrelson

Release Date: 15 January 2009
