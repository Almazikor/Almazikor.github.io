The Pursuit of Happyness

Storyline

Based on a true story about a man named Christopher Gardner.
Gardner has invested heavily in a device known as a "bone density
scanner". He feels like he has it made selling these devices. However, they do not
sell well as they are marginally better than x-ray at a much higher price. As Gardner
works to make ends meet, his wife leaves him and he loses his apartment. Forced to
live out in the streets with his son, Gardner continues to sell bone density
scanners while concurrently taking on an unpaid internship as a stockbroker, with slim
chances for advancement to a paid position. Before he can receive pay, he needs
to outshine the competition through 6 months of training, and to sell his devices
to stay afloat.

Director: Gabriele Muccino

Writer: Steve Conrad

Stars: Will Smith, Thandie Newton, Jaden Smith

Release Date: 22 March 2007
